WEBSITE FROM SCRATCH PROJECT

Overview:
Choose a real or fictional business and create a website completely from scratch.
The project is divided into THREE parts:
1. Content
2. Design
3. Interactivity

You will plan and justify your choices before implementation.

PART 1: CONTENT (Answer ALL questions, add your own if necessary)

The name of the business is PCB or (Personal Computer Builder).
2. Is the business real or fictional?
This is a Fictional business I have created.
3. What products or services does the business offer?

The Features and main goal of the website must be immediately visible on the homepage.
